## Why MyFreeCams Downloader

MyFreeCams is a live-first platform, which means the most important content is time-sensitive. Generic downloaders often miss the active media, and standard video-saving tricks do not help when the stream is being delivered dynamically in the browser.

MyFreeCams Downloader is built around that problem. It focuses on supported MyFreeCams pages, detects the available stream in your browser session, and gives you a more direct way to keep accessible content as MP4 without leaving the browser.

## Troubleshooting

**The extension is not detecting the stream**  
Press play first and wait a few seconds so the stream has time to initialize.

**The page control is missing**  
Open the extension popup directly. Some supported pages work better through the popup UI.

**Only one quality option is listed**  
That usually means the page is exposing a single playable stream variant.

**The recording stopped too early**  
Check whether the live session ended or your internet connection dropped during capture.

**The page requires account access**  
The extension only works on media you can already open and play in your active browser session.
